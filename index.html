History of Sagar

  • 🏛️ Ancient period – Region was part of the Chedi kingdom in central India.
  • 👑 Early medieval era – Ruled by Ahir Rajas with capital at Gadpahra.
  • ⚔️ Various local dynasties controlled the region over time.
  • 🏰 1660 – Modern Sagar founded by Udan Shah with a fort settlement.
  • 🐎 18th century – Came under Maratha rule (Peshwas).
  • 🏙️ Govindpant Bundele developed Sagar as an administrative center.
  • 🌊 City expanded around Sagar Lake and gained importance.
  • 🇬🇧 1818 – British East India Company took control after Maratha defeat.
  • 📜 1861 – Became part of the Central Provinces under British rule.
  • 🇮🇳 Post-1947 – Integrated into Madhya Pradesh after independence.
